The purpose of this site is to introduce a strategy for collaborative working to produce a web application. In this case the desired result is a "to-do" application. We also discuss a breakdown of the required features of the app and the most suitable areas of study covering the skills needed.

A list of potential features for the app are listed below and there are links on the left to pages that cover the points in more detail.
Security.
Registration and login features with validation.
A page for chat options.
A simple game.
Third party API (like a map)
