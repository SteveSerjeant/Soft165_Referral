The idea of the game is to provide a short distraction as and when required by the user. The range of potential subjects is quite large covering a variety of genres. It could be a simple crossword, hangman, or even something like battleships. It doesn’t even have to be a game, other considerations could be simple colour by number puzzles or brain-teasers. The choice is endless.

Naturally the most obvious student for this aspect of the application would be studying some form of computing with gaming but this does exclude co-operation from others. As has been shown throughout this would not be the topic for a single person,a consensus would be needed from the group as to the type and complexity of game required.
