The registration and login aspects of the application will require consideration to a number of different areas to restrict access to unauthorised users while protecting users and their data. First of all will be guidance and restrictions to ensure any usernames chosen are suitable, unique and inoffensive. Passwords will need to adhere to rules regarding length, mixing the use of letters, numbers and special characters and the avoidance of commonly used passwords. An element of appropriate encryption will be advisable to protect the passwords and user data.

A student studying computing or computing with information security will have sufficient knowledge and skills to fulfil these requirements thereby ensuring a safe experience for the user. Again as previously mentioned close co-operation with other team members would be strongly advised.
